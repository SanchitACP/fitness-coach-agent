# Future Work

Features intentionally **out of scope** for the capstone MVP, captured here to
show vision without scope creep.

- **Google Calendar sync** — push workouts to the user's calendar (Phase 2 / Sprint 2b).
- **Preference feedback loop** — "skip beef this week" re-runs the filter with
  updated constraints, mid-conversation.
- **Periodization & autoregulation** — progress the program over weeks.
- **Live recipe APIs** — replace the curated DB with a live nutrition source.
- **Preference learning** — learn likes/dislikes from feedback over time.
- **Web / mobile UI** — a friendlier front end than the CLI.
- **Progress tracking** — log weight, lifts, and adherence over time.
- **Injury modifications** — adapt workouts around limitations.
